Parts Used

Chassis: Steel frame

Engine: Typically a 150cc to 155cc, four-stroke, single-cylinder engine

Transmission: 5-speed gearbox

Wheels: Cast aluminum wheels

Tires: Tubeless tires

Suspension: Telescopic forks (front), monoshock (rear)

Brakes: Disc brakes (front and rear)

Fuel Tank: Capacity varies, typically around 7 liters

Seat: Single-piece seat for rider and passenger

Handlebars: Typically clip-on handlebars for a sporty riding position

Footpegs: Designed for a comfortable riding position

Instruments: Speedometer, tachometer, fuel gauge, indicator lights

Swingarm: Cast aluminum or steel, depending on the model

Bodywork: Fairings, fenders, etc., usually made of plastic or fiberglass

Electrical System: Battery, starter motor, ignition system, lighting system

Exhaust System: Exhaust pipe, muffler

Air Intake System: Air filter, carburetor/fuel injection system

Drivetrain: Chain, sprockets
